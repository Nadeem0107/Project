Our Story

Al-Marhaba Family Restaurant was founded in 2008 with a simple vision: to serve rich, authentic non-vegetarian cuisine in a warm, family-friendly setting. What started as a humble eatery with a few tables and a big dream has now grown into a beloved destination for food lovers across the city.

Inspired by traditional Mughlai, Arabian, and South Indian flavors, our chefs blend age-old recipes with modern techniques to bring you dishes that are bold, satisfying, and unforgettable. From our signature Chicken Mandi to sizzling tandoori platters, every bite tells a story of tradition, taste, and trust.

At Al-Marhaba, we believe that good food brings people together. Our spacious dining area, attentive staff, and a menu curated with love make every visit special—whether it’s a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a festive gathering.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We’re not just serving meals—we’re serving memories.
